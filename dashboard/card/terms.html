MediTrust Card - Terms & Conditions

By applying for the MediTrust Insurance Card, you agree to the following terms and conditions:

1. Eligibility

The MediTrust Card is available to Indian residents who have submitted accurate and complete personal and family details.

2. Data Use

All personal information provided will be securely stored and used only for the purpose of insurance verification and card issuance.

3. Insurance Limit

The insurance amount is pre-decided by the system and ranges between ₹5,000 to ₹20,000, depending on application status and verification.

4. Approval & Rejection

Submission of the form does not guarantee approval. MediTrust reserves the right to accept or reject any application based on verification.

5. Card Validity

The MediTrust Card is valid for use only within the MediTrust Hospital network. It is non-transferable and can only be used by the cardholder.

6. Misuse

Providing false information or misusing the card may result in cancellation of the card and legal action as per hospital policy.

7. Changes

MediTrust reserves the right to change these terms at any time without prior notice.
